define Gyro Turn No Reset Left Wheel Speed Right Wheel Speed Degree Greater/Less
start moving at Left Wheel Speed Right Wheel Speed % speed
if (Greater/Less = >) then
   wait until (yaw angle > Degree)
if (Greater/Less = <) then
   wait until (yaw angle < Degree)
if (Greater/Less = =) then
   wait until (yaw angle = Degree)
stop moving

define Gryo Turn Left Power Right Power Degree
set yaw angle to 0
start moving at Left Power Right Power % speed
wait until ((abs of yaw angle) > Degree)
