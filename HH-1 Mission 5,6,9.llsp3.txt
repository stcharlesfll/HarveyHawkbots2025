define Gyro turn left wheel speed right wheel speed turn degrees >/</=
start moving at left wheel speed right wheel speed % speed
if (>/</= = >) then
   wait until (yaw angle > turn degrees)
if (>/</= = <) then
   wait until (yaw angle < turn degrees)
if (>/</= = =) then
   wait until (yaw angle = turn degrees)
stop moving
